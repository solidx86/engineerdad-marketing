Fee Drag

The fee you don't feel

2.0% vs 0.5%: ~3× more lost to fees

A fee never shows up on a statement, but it compounds against you the same way returns compound for you: over 30 years a 2.0% annual fee quietly erases about 3× what a 0.5% fee does.

Illustrative compound-cost math only — RM300/month contributed monthly over 30 years at 6% gross annual return; each value is the future value of the annuity at the gross rate minus the same annuity at the net-of-fee rate, rounded.
