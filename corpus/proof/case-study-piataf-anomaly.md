# PIATAF — The Mixed-Asset Anomaly Worth Reverse-Engineering

> Public Islamic Asia Tactical Allocation Fund (PIATAF) is the EngineerDad archive's go-to example of an MFR anomaly worth a deeper read. Use as proof of methodology, not as a "buy this fund" pitch.

## English

### The headline number
PIATAF — categorised as a Mixed Asset fund, Shariah-compliant — delivered a **30.11% 3-year annualised return**, as cited from its Public Mutual factsheet and surfaced in the February 2026 MFR carousel.

For a Mixed Asset fund, that is a striking number. Most mixed-asset funds run lower-volatility profiles than pure-equity, and three-year annualised returns above 25% in this category are unusual.

### Why this is "anomaly worth reverse-engineering"

The MFR brand voice rejects "best fund in the category" framing. Instead, it asks: *what combination of holdings, allocation moves, or one-off events produced this number?* That is the engineering posture — debug the result, don't just amplify it.

For PIATAF, the candidate explanations a fair reverse-engineering would consider:
- Allocation calls during the cited 3-year window (was the fund overweight a sector that ran very hot?)
- Currency moves on offshore Asia exposure
- Specific holdings that benefitted from a regional or sectoral re-rating
- Comparison to the fund's own benchmark — the alpha question, not just the absolute-return question

The point is not to declare the fund "great" or "lucky" in a public post. The point is to *publicly model* how a careful investor reads a striking number, and to point readers at the fund's own Master Prospectus and PHS for the substantive answer.

### Use in content

Anchors any post that:
- Walks through MFR methodology.
- Counters the "top 5 funds for 2026" finfluencer frame.
- Demonstrates engineer-voice rigour ("debug the headline before you trust it").

### Compliance discipline

When citing the 30.11% figure publicly:
- Always reference the factsheet date.
- Always state "past performance is not indicative of future performance".
- Never imply the fund will continue to deliver this return.
- Do not use this case as a recommendation. Educational only.

## Bahasa Malaysia

### Nombor utama
PIATAF — dikategorikan sebagai dana Aset Campuran, patuh Shariah — mencatatkan **pulangan 30.11% tahunan tiga tahun**, sebagaimana dipetik daripada lembaran fakta Public Mutual dan diketengahkan dalam karusel MFR Februari 2026.

### Mengapa ini "anomali yang berbaloi direkayasa balik"

Suara jenama MFR menolak bingkai "dana terbaik dalam kategori". Sebaliknya, ia bertanya: *apa kombinasi pegangan, gerakan peruntukan, atau peristiwa sekali sahaja menghasilkan nombor ini?*

Calon penjelasan untuk dipertimbangkan dengan adil:
- Gerakan peruntukan dalam tetingkap 3 tahun yang dipetik
- Pergerakan mata wang pada pendedahan Asia luar pesisir
- Pegangan spesifik yang mendapat manfaat re-rating serantau atau sektoral
- Perbandingan dengan benchmark dana itu sendiri

- Sentiasa rujukkan tarikh lembaran fakta.
