# PRS Basics — Private Retirement Scheme

> Voluntary retirement supplement to EPF/KWSP. Targets `pre_retirement_prs_focus`, `business_owner_self_employed`, `salaried_professional_top_up` personas. Authored from the EngineerDad Compliance Rulebook + canonical EPF baseline tiers.

## English

### What PRS is
The Private Retirement Scheme (PRS) is a voluntary, long-term retirement-savings vehicle regulated by the Securities Commission Malaysia and the Private Pension Administrator (PPA). It supplements — it does not replace — your EPF/KWSP savings. Public Mutual is one of the licensed PRS providers; Solid is licensed as a PRS Consultant in addition to UTC.

### Why PRS matters (the gap PRS closes)

The EPF Baseline Reality Matrix (KWSP *Belanjawanku*-derived):
- **Basic**: RM390,000
- **Adequate**: RM650,000
- **Enhanced**: RM1,300,000

Most Malaysians don't hit the Adequate tier on EPF alone. PRS is the structured way to close the gap voluntarily without locking your money into a private insurance plan.

For the self-employed (no mandatory EPF contribution), PRS is often the *only* tax-advantaged retirement vehicle available. For salaried professionals already maxing EPF, PRS is the second tier.

### Tax-relief mechanics

Contributions of up to **RM3,000 per year** to PRS qualify for personal income tax relief. The relief is currently scheduled to run through 2030 (verify the active year via LHDN before any campaign).

For a top-rate taxpayer, this can mean ~RM700+ in real tax savings per year on a RM3,000 contribution — alongside the underlying retirement-savings growth.

### Lock-in tradeoff (must be named)

PRS funds are subject to lock-in until age 55. Pre-55 withdrawals are permitted only for housing, healthcare, death/disability, or after one full year of contribution (and then with an 8% tax penalty on the withdrawal). The lock-in is a feature, not a bug — it forces the discipline that makes long-horizon compounding work — but the audience must be told upfront.

### How PRS sits in a portfolio

PRS is the *retirement* leg. It does not replace:
- Your child's education fund (separate vehicle, separate horizon).
- Your liquid emergency fund (PRS is locked).
- Your unit-trust accumulation outside PRS (which has full liquidity).

A common structure for the dual-income parent:
- EPF — mandatory salary deduction.
- Unit trust (children's fund) — RM200–500/month per child via auto-debit.
- PRS — RM250/month (RM3,000/year) for the tax relief, on top of EPF.
- Liquid savings / FD — 3–6 months of household expenses.

### Who PRS is most useful for (persona reads)

- **`business_owner_self_employed`** — no mandatory EPF; PRS is often the primary structured retirement vehicle.
- **`pre_retirement_prs_focus`** — last decade of work; PRS catch-up + tax relief while income is still peaking.
- **`salaried_professional_top_up`** — already in EPF; PRS is the tax-advantaged top-up tier.

### What PRS is *not*

- **Not** a guaranteed-return product.
- **Not** "safer than EPF" — it is a different vehicle with different risk and different liquidity. Comparing PRS to EPF on safety is regulator-banned framing.
- **Not** a substitute for understanding which PRS fund inside the scheme matches your risk profile. PRS schemes typically offer Conservative / Moderate / Growth options. The matching exercise is exactly the same as for any unit trust — done one-on-one with the licensed Consultant.

## Bahasa Malaysia

### Apa itu PRS
Skim Persaraan Swasta (PRS) ialah kenderaan simpanan persaraan jangka panjang yang sukarela, dikawal selia oleh Suruhanjaya Sekuriti Malaysia dan Pentadbir Pencen Swasta (PPA). Ia *menambah* — bukan menggantikan — simpanan EPF/KWSP anda.

### Mengapa PRS penting

Tahap *Belanjawanku* EPF:
- **Asas**: RM390,000
- **Mencukupi**: RM650,000
- **Ditingkatkan**: RM1,300,000

Kebanyakan Malaysian tidak mencapai tahap Mencukupi melalui EPF sahaja. PRS ialah cara berstruktur untuk menutup jurang itu secara sukarela.

### Mekanik pelepasan cukai

Sumbangan sehingga **RM3,000 setahun** kepada PRS layak untuk pelepasan cukai pendapatan peribadi. Pelepasan ini dijadualkan untuk berlangsung sehingga 2030 (sahkan tahun aktif melalui LHDN sebelum mana-mana kempen).

### Pertukaran lock-in

Dana PRS tertakluk kepada lock-in sehingga umur 55. Pengeluaran sebelum 55 hanya dibenarkan untuk perumahan, penjagaan kesihatan, kematian/keilatan, atau selepas satu tahun penuh sumbangan (dengan penalti cukai 8% atas pengeluaran).

### Bagaimana PRS dalam portfolio

PRS ialah kaki *persaraan*. Ia tidak menggantikan dana pendidikan anak, dana kecemasan cair, atau pengumpulan amanah saham di luar PRS.

Struktur biasa untuk ibu bapa dwi-pendapatan:
- EPF — potongan gaji wajib.
- Amanah saham (dana anak) — RM200–500/bulan setiap anak melalui auto-debit.
- PRS — RM250/bulan (RM3,000/tahun) untuk pelepasan cukai.
- Simpanan cair / FD — 3–6 bulan perbelanjaan rumah tangga.

### PRS *bukan*
- **Bukan** produk pulangan dijamin.
- **Bukan** "lebih selamat daripada EPF" — ia kenderaan berbeza dengan risiko dan kecairan berbeza.
- **Bukan** pengganti memahami dana PRS mana yang sepadan dengan profil risiko anda.
