### Taxonomy notes

PIATAF is the representative **Shariah mixed-asset / tactical allocation** entry in the fund family. The key distinction between this fund and a pure equity fund is the mandate flexibility: a tactical allocation fund can in theory hold zero equity if the manager's view warrants it. In the April 2026 snapshot the fund is running near-maximum equity exposure, which means its short-term risk profile resembles an equity fund rather than a classic 60/40 balanced fund. Clients selecting this fund for "balanced" risk exposure should be made aware that the actual equity weight can differ substantially from category assumptions — verifying the current allocation in the factsheet is essential.
