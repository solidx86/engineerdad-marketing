# reel-9x16-visual-diagram-002

**Tone:** Neutral-authoritative, light (`--offwhite`) background.
**Demonstrates:** A concept-only 9:16 card built around a single big qualitative stat-callout — "Time > Timing" (NO numbers) — reinforced by a compact labelled 3-step diagram that resolves on an orange-accented "Let it compound". One dominant idea, one accent color, generous whitespace.
**Why it's a good exemplar:** Shows the "one qualitative idea, hero-typeset" reel pattern for hook/closing frames where a chart would be overkill. Demonstrates restraint, the Playfair/Inter hierarchy at hero scale, and safe-area discipline.
