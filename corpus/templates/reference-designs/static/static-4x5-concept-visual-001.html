The Snowball

It's the hill,
not the push

You don't need a bigger push — you need a longer hill. A modest amount, left to roll for years, gathers far more from time than from the size of the first scoop.

Illustrative of the compounding / dollar-cost-averaging principle in corpus/courses/compounding-dca.md (the brand's "Snowball" metaphor). A conceptual illustration of how time compounds a habit, not a projection of returns — actual outcomes depend on markets, fund choice, and consistency. Not a recommendation.
