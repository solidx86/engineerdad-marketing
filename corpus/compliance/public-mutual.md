### Brand & visual identity rules

#### Brand separation (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL)

- The **"EngineerDad" persona** must remain visually distinct from the Public Mutual corporate entity.
- The consultant's personal channel is **not** a Public Mutual channel; it must not impersonate, emulate, or resemble Public Mutual's corporate brand.
- Bio language must clearly identify the consultant as a **licensed Public Mutual Unit Trust Consultant** while keeping the channel itself unmistakably personal.

#### Logo usage (CONFIRMED + CONSULTANT-INTERNAL)

- **Never** use the official logos of Public Mutual, FIMM, SC, or KWSP/EPF as profile pictures, avatars, or integrated into custom channel banners. (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL — UTC Connect baseline; verify exact form.)
- **FIMM logo** is governed by FIMM CoE ¶4.3(n): only permissible to disclose membership/registration; any other use requires FIMM's prior written consent. (CONFIRMED.)
- **Public Mutual logo** usage by consultants is restricted to contexts and formats expressly permitted by Public Mutual UTC Connect. (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL — confirm with Public Mutual the specific UTC Connect rule and any approved-asset library.)
- **SC logo** must never appear in a consultant ad in a way that implies SC endorsement (SC §8.16–§8.18, FAQ Example 6). (CONFIRMED.)

#### Title (CONFIRMED — FIMM CoE ¶4.1(h) + UTC Connect)

- Permissible: "Public Mutual Unit Trust Consultant", "PRS Consultant", "UTC", "FIMM-registered Consultant".
- Banned: "Financial Planner", "Financial Advisor", "Investment Advisor", "Investment Consultant" — unless the consultant separately holds a CMSRL.
- Mandatory bio inclusion: FIMM registration number + Public Mutual Unit Trust Consultant title (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL — UTC Connect baseline; FIMM CoE ¶3.24 anchors this).

### Permitted product scope (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL + CONFIRMED via Public Mutual product range)

- **In scope:** Public Mutual unit trust funds (conventional + Shariah-compliant), Public Mutual PRS schemes (conventional + Shariah-compliant), KWSP i-Invest investments via Public Mutual unit trusts.
- **Out of scope** (cannot be promoted under the licensed identity): forex, cryptocurrencies, individual stock-picking, day-trading, margin/CFD products, structured products outside Public Mutual's range, foreign-broker products, alternative-investment platforms not authorised by SC.
- **FIMM CoE ¶4.2** anchors this: a Consultant must not deal in products they are not registered to market and distribute, or any product on the SC's alert list. (CONFIRMED.)

### Fund-name and risk-classification handling (INFERRED + CONSULTANT-INTERNAL)

- When referencing a Public Mutual fund publicly, use the **full official fund name** as it appears in the Master Prospectus / PHS (e.g., "Public Mutual Strategic Balanced Fund", not "Strategic Balanced"). First mention should be the full name; subsequent mentions can use a clearly-defined abbreviation.
- **Risk-classification labelling** is required wherever a fund's category is referenced — Cash / Bond / Mixed / Equity / Balanced / Sukuk / Money-Market — together with the official **Fund Volatility Class** (Very Low / Low / Moderate / High / Very High) per the Lipper Volatility Bands cited in the PHS.
- For **Shariah-compliant** funds, use the official "Public Islamic" / Shariah branding consistently; never imply that a non-Shariah fund is Shariah-compliant.
- **Sophisticated-investor funds** (e.g., funds offered under the SC's Lodge-and-Launch framework, addressed in Public Mutual's PB- / PRS- branded sophisticated-investor schemes) **must not** be promoted on open social media (CONFIRMED via SC §8.01 / Appendix 1 §A1).

### Performance-data sources (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL + CONFIRMED)

- **Only quote returns from official sources** — Public Mutual Master Prospectus, Supplemental Prospectus, Product Highlights Sheet (PHS), or official Fund Fact Sheet (the monthly factsheet published on publicmutual.com.my). (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL — UTC Connect rule, mirrored by FIMM CoE ¶4.6(b).)
- **Custom screeners or spreadsheets** (e.g., the Alpha-filtered Monthly Fund Review on engineerdad.my) are derivative analyses. They must be framed as **educational market analysis**, not as a "buy list" or recommendation. (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL.)
- **Lipper / Morningstar third-party ratings** must be sourced from credible publications and explained per SC App.1 §I3–§I5 (meaning, scale, source, currentness). The Investment Disclaimer reminds investors not to rely solely on ratings or rankings. (CONFIRMED.)
- **Footnote / disclaimer** on every performance reference: "Past performance of a fund is not a reliable indicator of its future performance." (CONFIRMED.)

### Suitability & one-on-one rule (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL + CONFIRMED via FIMM ¶3.7 / SC §8.01)

- Specific fund recommendations are reserved for **private one-on-one consultations** following a documented Suitability Assessment Form (SAF).
- Public-channel content must not function as a sales-pipeline shortcut around the SAF process. Posts that name a specific fund + prompt action ("invest now", "buy this fund", "this is the best") breach §8.01.
- The consultant's contact and consultation booking flow may be promoted publicly, provided the call to action is for a private consultation, not for direct subscription.

### Cash / payment handling (CONFIRMED — Public Mutual Master Prospectus)

Every Public Mutual prospectus carries the consumer-protection warning that the consultant **MUST NOT** receive cash or have client money paid into a personal account:

> DO NOT PAY CASH TO A UNIT TRUST SCHEME CONSULTANT OR STAFF OF PUBLIC MUTUAL.
>
> DO NOT MAKE DEPOSIT OR TRANSFER INTO THE BANK ACCOUNT OF A UNIT TRUST SCHEME CONSULTANT OR STAFF OF PUBLIC MUTUAL.
>
> DO NOT ISSUE A CHEQUE IN THE NAME OF A UNIT TRUST SCHEME CONSULTANT OR STAFF OF PUBLIC MUTUAL.
>
> Public Mutual will not be liable for any loss incurred should you give cash to our unit trust scheme consultant or staff.

This text appears in Public Mutual prospectus PDFs (sourced via search hits on the Fund Prospectus pages). Reproduce or echo this in any post that involves payment handling — and make absolutely sure consultant content never invites direct payment to the consultant.

### Social-media policy (INFERRED + CONSULTANT-INTERNAL)

The published Public Mutual disclaimers (above) cover ads disseminated through "printed or electronic, digital or other means of communications" — i.e., the entire footprint of social media is in scope. (CONFIRMED.)

Beyond that, the operational rule layered by UTC Connect (CONSULTANT-INTERNAL — verify with Public Mutual):

- **Pre-publication review** — every consultant-led promotional asset must be reviewed by the Distributor before publication. FIMM CoE ¶4.4(c) anchors this rule (Consultant cannot use materials not provided or approved by the Distributor).
- **No paid-message screenshots** of client conversations — even with names blurred (PDPA + FIMM CoE ¶4.7).
- **No direct fund-pitch livestreams** without a corporate-approved script + disclaimers continuously visible.
- **No paid-promotion arrangements with finfluencers** unless the Distributor approves and the influencer signs a compliance declaration (FIMM GN ¶3.2). (CONFIRMED.)
- **Repost / reshare** of Public Mutual corporate content is generally permitted but inherits SC §7.01 third-party-provider responsibility — the consultant should add a personal credential/disclaimer overlay to keep their target audience clearly informed.

### Pengendalian nama dana & klasifikasi risiko

- Gunakan **nama dana rasmi penuh** seperti dalam Master Prospectus / PHS pada sebutan pertama.

- Untuk dana **patuh Syariah**, gunakan jenama "Public Islamic" / Syariah secara konsisten.
- **Dana pelabur canggih** tidak boleh dipromosikan di media sosial terbuka (SC §8.01 / App.1 §A1).

### Sumber data prestasi

- Hanya sumber rasmi — Master Prospectus, PHS, Helaian Fakta Dana rasmi.
- Penapis tersuai = analisis pendidikan, bukan senarai beli (FIMM CoE ¶4.6(b)).
- Penilaian Lipper / Morningstar daripada penerbitan kredibel; jelaskan makna, skala, sumber, ketersediaan terkini (SC App.1 §I3–§I5).
- Penafian "Prestasi lalu bukan petunjuk yang boleh dipercayai bagi prestasi masa depan" pada setiap rujukan prestasi.
