# EPF Shortfall — Worked Cases

> Four concrete shortfall scenarios for hooks, carousel cards, and reels. Every figure is a literal lookup from `corpus/data/datasets/epf-sustainability-simulations.json` (item G) — the cached output of the EPF Sustainability Calculator — or from `corpus/data/datasets/kwsp-ria-benchmarks.json` (item C). This file does no arithmetic of its own; it narrates the rows. See `corpus/courses/epf-sustainability-model.md` for how the calculator produces these numbers.
> All projections are illustrative model output at the calculator's v1.3.2 default assumptions (5% dividend, 3% salary growth, 4% inflation, retire 60, life expectancy 80). They are not personalised advice and not a forecast of any individual's result.

## English

### How to read these cases

Each case runs one worker to retirement at 60. The *projected balance* is large and nominal — ringgit decades from now. The *need* is the same lifestyle priced in those future ringgit, so inflation has already been applied to it. The **gap** is the monthly distance between the two at retirement. The **years sustainable** figure is how long the balance lasts if drawn at that lifestyle — when it falls short of life expectancy (80), that is the shortfall made concrete.

The lifestyle tiers map to the calculator's income-replacement input: **Basic** = 50% of final working income, **Adequate** = 70%, **Enhanced** = 100%.

### Case 1 — The young saver · age 25 · RM3,000/month

- **Starting EPF:** RM18,000 — the age-25 benchmark balance (item C).
- **Projected EPF at 60:** RM1,247,032; monthly payout RM4,733.
- **Monthly gap** — Basic RM1,186 · Adequate RM3,554 · Enhanced RM7,105.
- At the Adequate lifestyle the balance lasts ~11.9 years — it would run dry around age 72.
- **To close the Adequate gap:** about **RM408/month** invested from now to 60 at the model's 8% assumption.

*Framing:* Even a million-ringgit projection leaves a gap once inflation is in the picture — but at 25, the fix is small. RM408/month is the cost of starting early; 35 years of compounding does the rest of the work.

### Case 2 — Early mid-career · age 30 · RM5,000/month

- **Starting EPF:** RM35,000 — the age-30 benchmark (item C).
- **Projected EPF at 60:** RM1,458,597; monthly payout RM5,536.
- **Monthly gap** — Basic RM2,573 · Adequate RM5,816 · Enhanced RM10,681.
- At the Adequate lifestyle the balance lasts ~10.2 years — running dry around age 70.
- **To close the Adequate gap:** about **RM1,028/month** at the 8% assumption.

*Framing:* Five years later the same gap costs more than twice as much per month to close. Still very manageable on a RM5,000 income — the point is that the price of the fix rises every year it is deferred.

### Case 3 — The mid-career wake-up · age 40 · RM10,000/month

- **Starting EPF:** RM86,000 — the age-40 benchmark (item C).
- **Projected EPF at 60:** RM1,397,301; monthly payout RM5,303.
- **Monthly gap** — Basic RM5,652 · Adequate RM10,035 · Enhanced RM16,608.
- At the Adequate lifestyle the balance lasts ~7.4 years — running dry around age 67.
- **To close the Adequate gap:** about **RM4,489/month** at the 8% assumption.

*Framing:* This is the wake-up case. RM4,489/month is close to half of a RM10,000 income — still possible if it starts now, but no longer comfortable. The gap between Case 1 and Case 3 is the price of fifteen years of "later".

### Case 4 — The late-career reckoning · age 50 · RM15,000/month

- **Starting EPF:** RM175,000 — the age-50 benchmark (item C).
- **Projected EPF at 60:** RM874,962; monthly payout RM3,321.
- **Monthly gap** — Basic RM7,781 · Adequate RM12,222 · Enhanced RM18,883.
- At the Adequate lifestyle the balance lasts ~4.6 years — running dry around age 65.
- **To close the Adequate gap:** the model puts the required top-up at about **RM17,602/month** — more than the entire RM15,000 salary.

*Framing:* This is the honest one. With only ten years left, contributions alone cannot close an Adequate-tier gap — the maths asks for more than the paycheck. The real conversation here is not a product; it is timeline (working past 60), lifestyle expectations (planning to the Basic tier rather than Adequate), and accepting there is no purely-mathematical rescue. Pretending otherwise would be dishonest.

### What the four cases say together

The cost of closing the same Adequate gap runs roughly RM408 → RM1,028 → RM4,489 → "more than the salary" across ages 25, 30, 40, and 50. The lesson is not "EPF fails" — it is that EPF is one engine, an inflation-adjusted retirement is the bill, and the gap between them is cheapest to close when there is the most time. The instrument that closes it (PRS, unit-trust accumulation, or a mix) is a question for a one-to-one consultation, not a one-size answer.

### Compliance discipline

- Every projection here is illustrative model output, not personalised advice and not a guaranteed or assured result.
- The monthly top-up figures are the mathematical implication of each gap at an 8% modelling assumption — they are not a recommendation to buy any specific product or fund.
- No fund is named. The instrument that closes a gap is set in a consultation that audits the individual's full position.
- Cite KWSP / the calculator as the source. Never frame PRS or unit trusts as lower-risk than, or certain to outperform, EPF.

## Bahasa Malaysia

### Cara membaca kes ini

Setiap kes menjalankan seorang pekerja hingga bersara pada 60. *Baki yang diunjurkan* adalah besar dan nominal — ringgit beberapa dekad dari sekarang. *Keperluan* ialah gaya hidup yang sama dihargakan dalam ringgit masa depan itu, jadi inflasi sudah pun digunakan padanya. **Jurang** ialah jarak bulanan antara kedua-duanya semasa bersara. Angka **tahun mampan** ialah berapa lama baki bertahan jika dikeluarkan pada gaya hidup itu — apabila ia kurang daripada jangka hayat (80), itulah kekurangan yang dijelmakan.

Tahap gaya hidup dipetakan kepada input penggantian pendapatan kalkulator: **Asas** = 50% pendapatan kerja akhir, **Mencukupi** = 70%, **Ditingkatkan** = 100%.

### Kes 1 — Penyimpan muda · umur 25 · RM3,000/bulan

- **EPF permulaan:** RM18,000 — penanda aras baki umur-25 (item C).
- **Unjuran EPF pada 60:** RM1,247,032; bayaran bulanan RM4,733.
- **Jurang bulanan** — Asas RM1,186 · Mencukupi RM3,554 · Ditingkatkan RM7,105.
- Pada gaya hidup Mencukupi, baki bertahan ~11.9 tahun — akan kering sekitar umur 72.
- **Untuk menutup jurang Mencukupi:** kira-kira **RM408/bulan** dilaburkan dari sekarang hingga 60 pada andaian model 8%.

*Bingkai:* Walaupun unjuran sejuta ringgit, jurang tetap wujud apabila inflasi diambil kira — tetapi pada umur 25, penyelesaiannya kecil. RM408/bulan ialah kos memulakan awal; 35 tahun pengkompaunan melakukan selebihnya.

### Kes 2 — Awal pertengahan kerjaya · umur 30 · RM5,000/bulan

- **EPF permulaan:** RM35,000 — penanda aras umur-30 (item C).
- **Unjuran EPF pada 60:** RM1,458,597; bayaran bulanan RM5,536.
- **Jurang bulanan** — Asas RM2,573 · Mencukupi RM5,816 · Ditingkatkan RM10,681.
- Pada gaya hidup Mencukupi, baki bertahan ~10.2 tahun — kering sekitar umur 70.
- **Untuk menutup jurang Mencukupi:** kira-kira **RM1,028/bulan** pada andaian 8%.

*Bingkai:* Lima tahun kemudian, jurang yang sama menelan kos lebih dua kali ganda sebulan untuk ditutup. Masih boleh diurus pada pendapatan RM5,000 — intinya ialah harga penyelesaian meningkat setiap tahun ia ditangguh.

### Kes 3 — Sedar di pertengahan kerjaya · umur 40 · RM10,000/bulan

- **EPF permulaan:** RM86,000 — penanda aras umur-40 (item C).
- **Unjuran EPF pada 60:** RM1,397,301; bayaran bulanan RM5,303.
- **Jurang bulanan** — Asas RM5,652 · Mencukupi RM10,035 · Ditingkatkan RM16,608.
- Pada gaya hidup Mencukupi, baki bertahan ~7.4 tahun — kering sekitar umur 67.
- **Untuk menutup jurang Mencukupi:** kira-kira **RM4,489/bulan** pada andaian 8%.

*Bingkai:* Ini kes sedar-diri. RM4,489/bulan hampir separuh daripada pendapatan RM10,000 — masih boleh jika bermula sekarang, tetapi tidak lagi selesa. Jurang antara Kes 1 dan Kes 3 ialah harga lima belas tahun "nanti".

### Kes 4 — Perhitungan lewat kerjaya · umur 50 · RM15,000/bulan

- **EPF permulaan:** RM175,000 — penanda aras umur-50 (item C).
- **Unjuran EPF pada 60:** RM874,962; bayaran bulanan RM3,321.
- **Jurang bulanan** — Asas RM7,781 · Mencukupi RM12,222 · Ditingkatkan RM18,883.
- Pada gaya hidup Mencukupi, baki bertahan ~4.6 tahun — kering sekitar umur 65.
- **Untuk menutup jurang Mencukupi:** model meletakkan tambahan diperlukan pada kira-kira **RM17,602/bulan** — lebih daripada keseluruhan gaji RM15,000.

*Bingkai:* Inilah yang jujur. Dengan hanya sepuluh tahun berbaki, caruman sahaja tidak boleh menutup jurang tahap Mencukupi — matematik meminta lebih daripada gaji. Perbualan sebenar di sini bukan produk; ia ialah jangka masa (bekerja melepasi 60), jangkaan gaya hidup (merancang ke tahap Asas dan bukan Mencukupi), dan menerima bahawa tiada penyelamat semata-mata matematik.

### Apa yang empat kes ini katakan bersama

Kos menutup jurang Mencukupi yang sama berjalan kira-kira RM408 → RM1,028 → RM4,489 → "lebih daripada gaji" merentas umur 25, 30, 40, dan 50. Pengajarannya bukan "EPF gagal" — ia ialah bahawa EPF satu enjin, persaraan terlaras inflasi ialah bilnya, dan jurang antara keduanya paling murah ditutup apabila ada paling banyak masa. Instrumen yang menutupnya (PRS, pengumpulan amanah saham, atau campuran) ialah soalan untuk perundingan satu-dengan-satu.

### Disiplin pematuhan

- Setiap unjuran di sini ialah output model ilustrasi, bukan nasihat peribadi dan bukan hasil yang dijamin atau dipastikan.
- Angka tambahan bulanan ialah implikasi matematik setiap jurang pada andaian model 8% — ia bukan cadangan membeli mana-mana produk atau dana tertentu.
- Tiada dana dinamakan. Instrumen yang menutup jurang ditetapkan dalam perundingan yang mengaudit kedudukan penuh individu.
- Petik KWSP / kalkulator sebagai sumber. Jangan sekali-kali membingkai PRS atau amanah saham sebagai berisiko lebih rendah daripada, atau pasti mengatasi, EPF.
