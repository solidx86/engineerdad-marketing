# EPF Baseline Reality Matrix

> The three-tier retirement-savings benchmark used as the diagnostic frame for every EPF / PRS / retirement-shortfall post in the EngineerDad Money Psychology Series. Derived from KWSP's *Belanjawanku* methodology (or its successor framework — verify before campaign launch).

## English

### The three tiers
- **Basic — RM390,000** · the floor for a minimum-viable retirement at modest spending.
- **Adequate — RM650,000** · enough for a reasonable middle-class retirement.
- **Enhanced — RM1,300,000** · cushion for a comfortable retirement with margin.

These are the *target balances at retirement age* needed to sustain the spending profile each tier represents, given KWSP's modelled drawdown assumptions and life-expectancy estimates.

### Why this matrix matters

Most Malaysian EPF members — by KWSP's own published statistics — do not reach the Adequate tier through EPF alone. The structural shortfall is the central diagnostic frame for any "rethink your retirement" content.

The matrix gives EngineerDad audiences a concrete number to react to. "I have RM250,000 in EPF" reads abstractly until placed against "Basic is RM390,000." The gap turns into action — usually a PRS contribution, a unit-trust top-up, or both.

### How to use in content

The standard hook structure:
1. Open with the matrix as the data point: *"KWSP's own framework defines retirement adequacy in three tiers — RM390k, RM650k, RM1.3M."*
2. Diagnose: *"Most Malaysians don't hit Adequate on EPF alone."*
3. Mechanism: *"The gap is real. The question is what closes it."*
4. Action: PRS for the tax-relief leg; unit-trust accumulation for the liquid leg; the audit happens in a one-on-one consultation.

Always cite KWSP / *Belanjawanku* as the source. Never present the tiers as EngineerDad numbers — they are KWSP-derived.

### Compliance discipline
- Frame as "EPF's own benchmark", not a personal recommendation.
- Never present as the *only* path — note that household needs, dependants, healthcare, and target retirement age all shift the actual figure.
- Never compare PRS/unit-trust performance to EPF dividends as "safer" or "guaranteed to beat" — that is regulator-banned framing.

## Bahasa Malaysia

### Tiga tahap
- **Asas — RM390,000** · paras untuk persaraan minimum berdaya maju pada perbelanjaan sederhana.
- **Mencukupi — RM650,000** · cukup untuk persaraan kelas menengah yang munasabah.
- **Ditingkatkan — RM1,300,000** · bantalan untuk persaraan selesa dengan margin.

### Mengapa matriks ini penting

Kebanyakan ahli EPF Malaysia — menurut statistik KWSP sendiri — tidak mencapai tahap *Mencukupi* melalui EPF sahaja.

### Bagaimana menggunakan dalam kandungan

1. Buka dengan matriks sebagai titik data.
2. Diagnos: *"Kebanyakan Malaysian tidak hit Mencukupi melalui EPF sahaja."*
3. Mekanisme: *"Jurang itu sebenar. Soalan ialah apa menutupnya."*
4. Tindakan: PRS untuk kaki pelepasan cukai; pengumpulan amanah saham untuk kaki cair.

Sentiasa petik KWSP / *Belanjawanku* sebagai sumber.

### Disiplin pematuhan
- Bingkaikan sebagai "benchmark EPF sendiri".
- Jangan persembahkan sebagai *satu-satunya* laluan.
- Jangan banding prestasi PRS/amanah saham dengan dividen EPF sebagai "lebih selamat".
