# Calculator Embed Spec

> Author guide for any Authority Article that links to or embeds the EPF Sustainability Calculator. It standardises the link, the call-to-action wording, the disclaimer that must sit above the embed, and the reflection prompt that should follow it. Use this whenever an article points a reader at the tool. EN only — this is an author-facing template, not consumer copy.

## Link pattern

- **Canonical URL:** `https://engineerdad.my/tools/epf-sustainability-calculator/`
- **Short link (social / share contexts):** `epf.engineerdad.my`
- Always link to the canonical URL from article body text. Reserve the short link for places where a tidy string matters (image captions, spoken scripts, reels).
- When tracking is needed, append a UTM query (`?utm_source=...&utm_medium=...&utm_campaign=...`) — never bake tracking into the canonical link stored in the corpus.
- Link on a descriptive anchor ("run your own EPF projection"), never on a bare URL or "click here".

## CTA copy — three variants

Pick the variant that matches the article's funnel stage. All three point at the same tool.

- **Soft (top of funnel):** "Curious where you actually stand? The EPF Sustainability Calculator is free and takes about two minutes — run your own numbers."
- **Medium (consideration):** "Enter your real age, income, and EPF balance and the calculator will show your projected depletion age — the age your EPF would run dry at your target lifestyle."
- **Hard (decision):** "If the calculator shows a gap, the results panel includes a free WhatsApp consultation with Shoo Kyuk Wei, a licensed Public Mutual UTC and PRS consultant. Bring your numbers to that conversation."

## Pre-embed disclaimer template

Place this immediately above the embed or link, every time:

> The calculator below is an educational projection tool. Every rate in it — EPF dividend, salary growth, inflation, investment return — is an adjustable modelling assumption, not a forecast. Results are illustrative, depend entirely on the inputs you enter, and are not personalised financial advice.

## Post-embed reflection prompt

Place a reflection prompt immediately after the embed to convert a calculation into a conversation. Rotate among:

- "Did your projected depletion age land before or after your life expectancy? That single number is the whole conversation."
- "Try it twice — once at your current contribution, once with a modest monthly top-up. The difference is what starting today is worth."
- "If the gap surprised you, it is usually inflation doing the work. A future-dated bill is bigger than today's bill."

## Author checklist

- [ ] Canonical URL used on a descriptive anchor.
- [ ] Pre-embed disclaimer present, verbatim, directly above the tool.
- [ ] Exactly one CTA variant, matched to funnel stage.
- [ ] A reflection prompt follows the embed.
- [ ] No projected figure from the calculator is presented as a guaranteed or assured outcome.
- [ ] The article still carries its own full compliance block — this spec governs the embed, not the whole article.
