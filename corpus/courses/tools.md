## English

### Live tool

#### EPF Sustainability Calculator *(BETA)*
- **URL**: https://engineerdad.my/tools/epf-sustainability-calculator/
- **Purpose** — "Estimate how long your EPF savings will last in retirement, and what you need to do about it."
- **Inputs** — current EPF balance · expected retirement age · expected monthly retirement spending · life expectancy assumption.
- **Assumptions baked in** — historical EPF dividend rates as the inflow during retirement, against monthly drawdown for living expenses. Inflation drag on real spending power is acknowledged via the *Belanjawanku*-style reality matrix (Basic RM390k / Adequate RM650k / Enhanced RM1.3M as the EPF baseline tiers).
- **Output** — projected age at which the EPF balance is exhausted, plus a "gap" figure showing how much additional savings (e.g. PRS, unit trust accumulation) would be needed to bridge the gap to a target retirement age.
- **Use in content** — the tool is the proof-of-capability artefact behind every "How much retirement fund is actually enough?" post. Drive readers to it from any retirement / EPF / PRS hook.

### Roadmap (named publicly, not yet live)

#### Education Fund Projector
- **Purpose** — model 18-year growth from a monthly contribution and an assumed return.
- **Expected inputs** — child's current age · target year of university enrolment · monthly contribution · assumed annual return · target amount.
- **Expected output** — projected end-of-horizon value, monthly contribution required to hit a target, and a year-by-year curve.
- **Anchors** — the canonical RM200/month from birth → RM100k+ at age 18 example. Education inflation 5–6% baked into the target-amount default.
- **Use in content** — the proof-of-capability behind every Children Fund 101 / 5-Step Education Fund piece. CTA target for any "what should I be saving?" hook.

#### Inflation Impact Calculator
- **Purpose** — show exactly how inflation erodes savings, and what real return is needed to beat it.
- **Expected inputs** — initial savings · monthly contribution · current bank/FD interest rate · assumed inflation rate · time horizon.
- **Expected output** — the *real* (inflation-adjusted) value of savings at the end of the horizon vs. the *nominal* value, plus the breakeven return required.
- **Use in content** — the diagnostic tool for the "FD is not safe — it's a guaranteed shortfall" frame. CTA target for any Three Gaps Gap-3 piece.

#### Cost of Waiting Calculator
- **Purpose** — compare starting today vs. waiting 1, 3, or 5 years. *"See the latency penalty in real numbers."*
- **Expected inputs** — monthly contribution · time horizon · assumed annual return · delay scenarios (1y, 3y, 5y).
- **Expected output** — side-by-side end-of-horizon values for each delay scenario, with the dollar amount lost to each year of delay.
- **Use in content** — the proof-of-capability behind every "The Latency Penalty" / "the best time was yesterday" piece. CTA target for any Children Fund 101 Module 1.4 hook.

## Bahasa Malaysia

### Alat aktif

#### Kalkulator Kelestarian EPF *(BETA)*
- **URL**: https://engineerdad.my/tools/epf-sustainability-calculator/
- **Tujuan** — "Anggar berapa lama simpanan EPF anda akan bertahan dalam persaraan, dan apa yang anda perlu lakukan."
- **Input** — baki EPF semasa · umur persaraan dijangka · perbelanjaan bulanan persaraan dijangka · andaian jangka hayat.
- **Andaian** — kadar dividen EPF sejarah sebagai aliran masuk semasa persaraan, terhadap pengeluaran bulanan. Tahap *Belanjawanku*: Asas RM390k / Mencukupi RM650k / Ditingkatkan RM1.3M.
- **Output** — umur dijangka di mana baki EPF habis, plus angka "jurang" yang menunjukkan simpanan tambahan diperlukan.

### Pelan jalan

#### Penunjuk Dana Pendidikan
- **Tujuan** — model pertumbuhan 18 tahun daripada sumbangan bulanan + andaian pulangan.
- **Andaian** — Inflasi pendidikan 5–6%, contoh kanonikal RM200/bulan dari kelahiran → RM100k+ pada usia 18.

#### Kalkulator Impak Inflasi
- **Tujuan** — tunjukkan dengan tepat bagaimana inflasi menghakis simpanan, dan pulangan sebenar yang diperlukan.

#### Kalkulator Kos Menunggu
- **Tujuan** — bandingkan bermula hari ini vs. menunggu 1, 3, atau 5 tahun. *"Lihat penalti kelewatan dalam nombor sebenar."*
